_A mod designed to improve the chunk performance of Minecraft._

> by ishland

Find Concurrent Chunk Management Engine (NeoForge) on [Modrinth](https://modrinth.com/mod/c2me-neoforge).

---

We use Concurrent Chunk Management Engine (NeoForge) to noticeably speed up chunk generation, and marginally improve chunk load times.

## Usage

Concurrent Chunk Management Engine (NeoForge) runs without any user interaction.

## Config

Concurrent Chunk Management Engine (NeoForge)'s defaults are sufficient so we provide no config overrides.
